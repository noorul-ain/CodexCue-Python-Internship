Plagiarism detection plays a crucial role in both academic and professional environments. As the accessibility of information grows, so does the ease of copying text without proper attribution, leading to concerns over originality and intellectual property. Plagiarism detection tools have become essential, providing educators, researchers, and employers with a way to verify the authenticity of written work. Using advanced techniques like natural language processing, machine learning, and algorithms such as cosine similarity, these tools analyze documents to identify overlapping content and assess similarity between texts. By detecting potential instances of plagiarism, such tools uphold academic integrity, encourage original thought, and help protect the value of unique contributions in any field
